GLOSSARY OF TERMS

HERE ARE SOME KEY TERMS IN AFRICAN TRADITIONAL RELIGION;


  • Libation: The act of pouring liquid as an offering to spirits or ancestors.

  • Orisha: Deities in the Yoruba religion.

  • Divination: The practice of seeking knowledge or guidance through spiritual means.

  • Abosom: Deities in Akan spirituality

  • Egungun: Ancestor spirits in Yoruba culture
